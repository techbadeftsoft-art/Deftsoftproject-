# Staff Mobile App: Installation Guide

The Staff Mobile App is a **Progressive Web App (PWA)**. This means it can be installed directly from your browser onto your phone or computer, providing a native app experience without needing an app store.

## 📱 How to Install on Mobile

### **Android (Google Chrome)**
1. Open the app URL in Chrome.
2. Wait for a few seconds. A button saying **"Install Staff App"** will appear in the **Settings (Setup tab)** of the app.
3. Alternatively, tap the **three dots (⋮)** in the top right corner of Chrome.
4. Select **"Install App"** or **"Add to Home screen"**.
5. Tap **Install** to confirm. 

### **iPhone / iPad (Safari)**
1. Open the app URL in Safari.
2. Tap the **Share** button (the square icon with an upward arrow at the bottom).
3. Scroll down the list of options and tap **"Add to Home Screen"**.
4. Tap **Add** in the top right corner.

---

## 💻 How to Install on Desktop

### **Google Chrome / Microsoft Edge**
1. Open the app URL in your browser.
2. Look at the **Address Bar** at the top.
3. On the far right of the address bar, you will see an **Install Icon** (it looks like a computer screen with a down arrow).
4. Click it and select **Install**.
5. The app will now open in its own window and appear in your Applications folder/Start menu.

---

## 💡 Benefits of Installing
- **Offline Access:** The app works even when you have no internet connection.
- **Native Experience:** No browser bars, full-screen view.
- **Fast Loading:** The app is cached locally on your device for instant startup.
- **Push Notifications:** Stay updated with task alerts directly on your device.

> [!TIP]
> If you don't see the "Install" button immediately, try refreshing the page or checking if your browser is up to date.
